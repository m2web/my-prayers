Today's Westminster Catechism Q/A

103 - What do we pray for in the third petition?
In the third petition, which is, Thy will be done in earth, as it is in heaven, we pray that God, by his grace, would make us able and willing to know, obey and submit to his will in all things, as the angels do in heaven.

Today's Request

Federal Government:
Lord, we lift up the three branches of the United States government and all who serve within them. For the Executive Branch, we pray for the President, the Vice President, the Cabinet, and all federal agencies—grant them wisdom, integrity, and a deep sense of accountability before You. For the Legislative Branch, we pray for members of Congress in both the House and the Senate—give them clarity, courage, and a heart to serve the public good rather than personal or partisan gain. For the Judicial Branch, we ask that judges at every level, especially the Supreme Court, would uphold justice with humility, impartiality, and fear of You. We also pray for special interest groups and the national media—that they would act with honesty, fairness, and a sincere desire to inform and not to manipulate. As Proverbs 21:1 says, "The king's heart is a stream of water in the hand of the LORD; he turns it wherever he will." May You guide their hearts according to Your perfect will.

Immediate Family:

We lift up Seth—for his salvation and well-being, and that he would be a loving and wise father to his children. For Emily, we ask for her salvation and for grace and strength in her parenting. We pray for Harley—for salvation, health, and protection. For Timothy and Luna, along with Bill and Angie, may their relationships be strong and grounded in Your love. For John and Alyse, we ask that their goals would be shaped by Your Word. Give them wisdom, restraint, and foresight in both home and work. We lift up Oliver, Remi, and Cassidy—for salvation, health, and safety. For Sophya, we pray for her salvation, safety, and well-being at home. For our entire family, we ask for hearts that are motivated to glorify You in everything. Help us all make wise choices in what we listen to and watch. Teach us to turn our fears, needs, and frustrations into faith in the Lord Jesus.

IOUS:
I come before You, confessing my heart's propensity to wander. I pray for Your Spirit to powerfully incline my heart to your testimonies, and not to selfish gain! (Psalm 119:36). Help me to value Your truth above all selfish gain. Turn my eyes from looking at worthless things; and give me life in your ways (Psalm 119:37). Empower me to find true life and joy in Your ways. I pray that You would open my eyes, that I may behold wondrous things out of your law (Psalm 119:18). Remove any veil from my heart and mind that prevents me from truly beholding the wondrous things in Your law. Grant me spiritual insight to grasp the depth of Your truth, that I may be amazed and transformed by Your Word. O LORD, I desire to walk in Your truth. Teach me your way, O LORD, that I may walk in your truth; unite my heart to fear your name (Psalm 86:11). Purify my motives and my affections so that my entire being is singularly devoted to fearing Your holy name. May there be no division in my loyalty, but a wholehearted commitment to You alone. Gracious God, I pray that You would satisfy us in the morning with your steadfast love, that we may rejoice and be glad all our days (Psalm 90:14). Let Your love be the deep well from which I draw all my contentment and joy. May this satisfaction in You empower me to rejoice and be glad in You all my days, regardless of my circumstances.
