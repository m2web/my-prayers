Today's Request

State Government:
Lord, we lift up the leaders of our states to You. We pray for Kentucky Governor Andy Beshear, Ohio Governor Mike DeWine, and Indiana Governor Mike Braun. Grant them wisdom, humility, and a desire to serve with integrity. We also pray for their Executive Cabinet and Committee Members—guide their decisions and surround them with wise counsel. For all state legislators, we ask for discernment, courage, and a heart to uphold justice and truth. We lift up the State Courts—may their rulings reflect righteousness and impartiality. For the State Militias and all Law Enforcement personnel, we ask for strength, protection, and accountability in their service. As Daniel 2:20–22 says, "Blessed be the name of God forever and ever, to whom belong wisdom and might. He changes times and seasons; he removes kings and sets up kings; he gives wisdom to the wise and knowledge to those who have understanding; he reveals deep and hidden things; he knows what is in the darkness, and the light dwells with him."

World Situation:
Lord, we lift up new believers in restricted areas. Give them wisdom and courage as they share their faith. Please ensure the safe delivery of Bibles into their hands. We ask You to powerfully work through media ministries that are broadcasting Your Word across closed borders. Let these messages reach hearts and bear lasting fruit. Strengthen and protect persecuted Christians, especially those imprisoned for their faith. Surround them with Your presence and sustain their hope. Stir the hearts of believers in free nations. Help them to embrace their faith deeply and actively support their persecuted brothers and sisters with prayer, advocacy, and generosity. We pray for the transformation of government officials in hostile nations. Soften their hearts and guide them toward truth, justice, and mercy. Bless and uphold pastoral leaders working in difficult and dangerous places. Give them endurance, joy, and the resources they need. And finally, Lord, we pray for the Pashtun peoples. Open their hearts to Your truth and raise up laborers to share the Good News with them.
